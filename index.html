Welcome to Little Bell Hotel

Little Bell Hotel brings beautiful bell tents to Sussex, Kent, and Surrey, so you can enjoy the great outdoors, without the hard work. We know camping can be a bit of a faff – peg wrestling, air bed gymnastics, and the eternal hunt for the missing mallet, so we’ve taken all that stress away. Just pick your perfect pitch, and we’ll bring the magic. 

Our bell tents are beautiful inside and out, with delightfully comfy, inviting interiors that will have you waking up on the right side of the bed – or should we say, the sunny side! Whether it’s a whole bell tent village for your wedding or milestone party, festival tents, or romantic retreat, we’ll create a stay that’s perfect for your event.

Book Your Bell Tents

All The Bells and Whistles (and then some)

Our process is designed to seamlessly manage bookings of any size, whether you’re hosting an intimate gathering or a full on festival style wedding. Just give us your date and the number of tents you need, we’ll take care of the rest. We liaise directly with your guests to organise everything: tent styles, number of beds, bedding options, and payment.

Inside each tent, you’ll find thoughtful touches and cosy comforts including plenty or warm and twinkly lights, soft rugs underfoot, and carefully chosen details that create a welcoming space. Our beds have been tried and tested (many times!) to ensure a great night’s sleep, and if you’d like to add bedding, we’ve got that covered too.

We like to keep things flexible, so if you have an idea or request, just ask – we’re quick to respond and always up for making the magic happen.
