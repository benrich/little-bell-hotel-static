About Us

We bend over backwards to ensure the perfect experience

Camping with us is all about creating those fun memories you’ll want to cherish. We love the extra special feeling of sleeping under the stars, and even better, doing it in a luxury cotton canvas bell tent with all the creature comforts. There’s nothing quite like it. But it’s not just about what’s in the tent. Our whole team is here to bring good energy, cheerful faces, and a stress-free experience from start to finish. We know your occasion is special and we treat it that way. We’ll bend over backwards to make it ‘pitch’ perfect, whatever the weather.

We started Little Bell Hotel out of a love for camping, the outdoors, and enjoying the countryside. Some of our favourite memories have made in a tent, that’s including all the mud, chaos and heavy lifting. But after years of testing every kind of camp bed, and doing battle with tents in the wind, we knew there had to be an easier way to keep fun and ditch the hassle.

Whether you’re a seasoned camper or completely new to it, we’ve put our experience into making it simpler to enjoy the fun of camping.

We’re a small team with a big love for helping people unwind, celebrate, and connect in beautiful places.

Frequently Asked Quesions

You’ll find answers to our most frequently asked questions below. If there’s something we haven’t covered, feel free to get in touch, we’re always happy to help and aim to be as accommodating as possible.

Can we choose where the tents go?

Absolutely! If you’re on private land or a venue with space, we’re happy to work with your preferred layout, we can even help with planning the best setup.

Do you set up and take down the tents?

Yes, all of our packages include full setup and take down at your chosen location. You just show up and enjoy!

Can guests book and pay for their own tents?

Yes, we offer a guest booking service to take the pressure off you. We’ll coordinate everything and keep you updated along the way.

Do you hire bell tents during the week?

Yes, absolutely. While weekends are our most popular, we’re happy to accommodate weekday bookings where availability allows, just drop us a message with your dates.

What happens if we damage a tent?

Our tents are sturdy and built to last, but if damage does occur, we’ll assess the situation. Minor wear and tear is usually no problem, but for significant damage, cleaning or repair fees may apply. We’re always fair and transparent about any costs.
