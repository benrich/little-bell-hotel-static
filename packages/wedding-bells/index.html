Frequently Asked Quesions

You’ll find answers to our most frequently asked questions below. If there’s something we haven’t covered, feel free to get in touch, we’re always happy to help and aim to be as accommodating as possible.

Can we choose where the tents go?

Absolutely! If you’re on private land or a venue with space, we’re happy to work with your preferred layout, we can even help with planning the best setup.

Do you set up and take down the tents?

Yes, all of our packages include full setup and take down at your chosen location. You just show up and enjoy!

Can guests book and pay for their own tents?

Yes, we offer a guest booking service to take the pressure off you. We’ll coordinate everything and keep you updated along the way.

Do you hire bell tents during the week?

Yes, absolutely. While weekends are our most popular, we’re happy to accommodate weekday bookings where availability allows,  just drop us a message with your dates.

What happens if we damage a tent?

Our tents are sturdy and built to last, but if damage does occur, we’ll assess the situation. Minor wear and tear is usually no problem, but for significant damage, cleaning or repair fees may apply. We’re always fair and transparent about any costs.
