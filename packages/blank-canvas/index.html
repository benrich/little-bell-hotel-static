Blank Canvas

Just the essentials. Beautifully simple bell tents, ready for you to make your own. Perfect as a blank canvas to add your own personal touches or style to suit your event.

Sleeps: Up to 6 Guests
Price: From £80

Enquire Now

Pick your perfect spot, tell us now many tents you need, and we will handle the rest – guest bookings, payments, and all the little details to ensure your perfect package!

What's included

A 100% cotton canvas bell tent with zipped in ground sheet, fully set up and taken down at your chosen location.

Inside your tent

Printed full moon flooring.

Outside your tent

Bunting and a doormat for a welcoming touch.

Optional Extras

Bedding packs – £20 per person, per night. Includes a luxury 13.5 tog duvet with cover, pillows, sheet, and cushions for a cosy night’s sleep.

You can also hire:
Deck chairs, directors chairs, additional festoon lighting for shared spaces, throws, ottomans, sheepskin rugs, and giant lawn games.
